Our Equipment
Top-tier equipment is key to both safety and performance. We provide high-quality gear that’s maintained regularly and suited for all levels—beginner to expert. From helmets and skis to sticks and pads, you’ll find everything you need to gear up confidently for every session.
Hockey Training
Fuel your performance with professional hockey training sessions. Our program covers skating techniques, puck control, defensive strategies, and goalie drills. Whether you're learning fundamentals or refining elite-level skills, our trainers and facilities are designed to help you thrive on the ice.
Private Lessons
Take your skills to the next level with personalized one-on-one training. Our certified instructors tailor each lesson to your pace and goals—perfect for beginners, athletes prepping for competition, or anyone looking for focused improvement. Flexible scheduling makes it easy to fit into your routine
Ski Touring
Explore the thrill of winter landscapes with our guided ski touring experiences. Whether you're a first-timer or a seasoned skier, our packages include expert guidance, safety equipment, and breathtaking trails. Custom tours are available on request to match your skill level and adventure goals.
Booking
Booking your session is simple and stress-free. Use our online platform to select your preferred date, time, and service. We recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance to secure your spot—especially during peak weekends and holidays. Need help? Our support team is just a message away.
Pricings
We believe in offering transparent and flexible pricing to suit all customer needs. Whether you're booking a single session or signing up for long-term packages, our pricing tiers are crafted to balance affordability with premium service. Group discounts, seasonal offers, and membership benefits are also available.
